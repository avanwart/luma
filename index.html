Frequently Asked Questions

How many Luma devices does my home need?

Three Luma devices will provide fast, reliable WiFi throughout the typical home.

How does a Luma wireless network operate?

The first Luma plugs into your modem to provide your WiFi network Internet access. Other Luma devices wirelessly connect to the first to surround your home in WiFi.

When will Luma ship?

Luma will ship in Spring 2016.

Can Luma keep my children safe when they’re not using my home WiFi network?

Yes. Luma includes features that allow you to manage and monitor devices even when they’re off your network.

What is the Luma return policy?

You can return Luma for a full refund within the first 15 days after you receive it.
